Super Simple Packing Checklist

Here’s a tiny list to make packing easy‑peasy. Print it, or save it on your phone.

Essentials

  • Wallet, ID, cards
  • Phone + charger + power bank
  • Medications (basic pain/fever relief, band‑aids)
  • Light jacket or shawl

Clothes (mix & match)

  • 2–3 tops + 2 bottoms
  • Undergarments + socks
  • Comfy walking shoes
  • Sleepwear

Toiletries (travel sizes)

  • Toothbrush + paste
  • Face wash + moisturizer + sunscreen
  • Comb/hair tie
